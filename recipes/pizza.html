Pizza Recipe




Pizza How-To

The following recipe is for making a pepperoni pizza. Please use all the indredients and follow all the instructions below for a delicious pizza!


Ingredients:

  • 10 ounces store-bought pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup store-bought pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese shredded
  • 2 ounces pepperoni slices
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal optional
  • flour for dusting when stretching dough

Instructions:

  1. Pizza dough should be at room temperature before you begin rolling it. If the dough has been kept in the refrigerator leave the dough on the counter for about twenty minutes before attempting to roll the dough.
  2. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Oil a baking sheet or add a handul of cornmeal to the pan per the instructions for the pizza dough.
  3. Gently stretch and knead the dough on a clean countertop. Add a sprinkle of flour as you stretch the dough if the dough becomes sticky. Add flour to the rolling pin and roll the pizza dough until it is twelve inches across.
  4. Transfer the dough to the baking pan or round pizza pan. Add tomato sauce, cheese and pepperoni.
  5. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes. The crust will be fluffy on the inside but golden brown on the outside when it has finished cooking. Remove from oven, slice and enjoy!
