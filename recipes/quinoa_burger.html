Red Bean n Mushroom Burger

PATTIES
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 4 ounces of cremini mushrooms, cleaned, stems removed, and finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons dried crushed red pepper flakes
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic (store-bought for ease of preparation)
- 1 can (15 ounces) low-sodium pinto beans, rinsed and drained
- 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

- 1/3 cup mayonnaise
- 1 1/2 teaspoons minced canned chipotle peppers in sauce
- 2 slices ripe tomatoes (per pattie or to your liking)
- 2 lettuce leaves (per pattie or to your liking)
- Extra-virgin olive oil (for brushing veggie patties)
- Hamburger buns of choice


INSTRUCTIONS
1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, warm the oil. Add the finely chopped mushrooms until tender, about 3 minutes of cooking. Add the crushed red peppers flakes, and minced garlic. Brown until garlic is lightly browned (be careful not to burn garlic)! Remove from heat.

2. Add cooked mushrooms and ingredients into blender. Add other pattie ingredients into blender. Blend ALL ingredients until coarsely chopped.

3. Empty out blender ingredients and form four patties of equal size, each about 1/2 inch thick. Refrigerate the patties until ready to grill.

4. In a separate bowl, combine the mayonnaise and chipotle peppers.

5. Prepare the grill for direct cooking over medium heat (350℉ to 450℉).

6. Brush the veggie patties on both sides with the extra-virgin olive oil. Grill the patties over direct medium heat, with the lid closed, until browned, 6 to 8 minutes. Flip only ONCE halfway through cooking time.

7. Optionally, hamburger buns can also be toasted to your liking.

8. Once patties are done, build each hamburger starting with the chili-mayonnaise on the bottom bun, lettuce leaves, tomatoe slices, veggie patty, and chili-mayonnaise on the top bun.

9. ENJOY!!!
