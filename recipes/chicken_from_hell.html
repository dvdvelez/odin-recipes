Spicy Chicken Burger from Hell

PATTIES
- 1 1/2 pounds ground chicken
- 1 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire suace
- 1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic (store-bought for ease of preparation)
- 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 4 large serrano peppers, finely diced (remove seeds)
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

- Kosher salt
- Ground black pepper
- Red onion slices (to your delight; optional)
- 1 slice ripe tomato (per pattie; optional)
- 2 lettuce leaves (per pattie or to your liking; optional)
- Favorite mayonnaise (to your delight; optional)
- Hamburger buns of choice
- Extra-virgin olive oil (for brushing chicken patties)
- Store-bought or homemade guacamole. Make sure to add 2 finely diced serrano peppers (remove seeds). Guacamole recipe can be found here


INSTRUCTIONS
1. Mix the patty ingredients, and then form four patties of equal size, each about 1 inch thick. With your thumb or the back of a spoon, make a shallow indentation about 1 inch wide in the center of the patties. This is to prevent the patties from forming a dome as they cook. Refrigerate the patties until ready to grill.

2. Prepare the grill for direct cooking over medium heat (350℉ to 450℉).

3. Brush the patties on both sides with the extra-virgin olive oil and lightly season with salt and pepper. Grill the patties over direct medium-high heat, with the lid closed, until FULLY cooked to (165℉) for a total of 11 to 13 minutes. Flip only ONCE halfway through cooking time.
Note: Chicken patties and chicken in general should be fully cooked. Fully-cooked meat will depend on thickness of the patty, heat of grill, distance of the patty from the direct heat of the grill, and other factors. Please adjust cooking times accordingly.
For additional cooking times, doneness, and health warnings click here.

4. Optionally, hamburger buns can also be toasted to your liking.

5. Once patties are done, build each hamburger starting with mayonnaise on the bottom bun, lettuce leaves, onion slices, tomato slice, chicken patty, generous amount of guacamole, and mayonnaise on the top bun.

6. ENJOY!!!
