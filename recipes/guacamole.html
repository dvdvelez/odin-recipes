Guacamole Recipe




Guacamole How-To

The following recipe is for making guacamole. Please use all the indredients and follow all the instructions below for a delicious bowl of guacamole!


Ingredients:

  • 3 Ripe avocados (cubed; no specific sized cubes. This is just to make it easier to mash the avocados).
  • 3/4 Cup tomatoes diced (about 3-4 roma tomatoes).
  • 1/4 Cup red onion diced (about 1/4 of a red onion).
  • 1/4 Cup cilantro or parsley (whichever you prefer).
  • 1 Jalape単o pepper. Wear gloves when slicing! Removing the seeds and the white membrane can keep the guacamole from becoming too spicy.
  • 2 Limes or 1 large lemon.
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt (can add more to preferred taste)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocados into large pieces. In a large bowl mash the avocados with the back of a fork or a potato masher. The more you mash the smoother the guacamole will be.
  2. Add the salt and squeeze the juice of both limes over the guacamole and mix. Not only does this add flavor but the lime juice can also help prevent the guacamole from turning brown. Taste and add more salt if desired.
  3. Dice the tomatoes, onions and cilantro.
  4. Use gloves to slice the Jalape単o Pepper. Remove any inner white membrane and seeds unless you preffer a spicer guacamole. Mince the Jalape単o into tiny pieces.
  5. Add the tomatoes, onions, cilantro and jalape単o pepper to the avocado and mix until combined. Serve and enjoy immediately!
